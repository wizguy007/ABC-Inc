ABOUT ABC Inc.

ABC Inc. is a global leading developer and publisher of free-to-play games for PC, Smartphones and Tablet devices.

We focused on creating compelling IP games such as WATCHDOGS, PROTOTYPE II, SPLINTERCELL, HITMAN and much more on a wide range of platforms including IOS, ANDROIDTM, WINDOWS PHONE and MAC OS.

Our unique technology platform enables its titles to be accessible to a broad audience of consumers globally.

Founded in 2016, ABC Inc. is headquarted in Nigeria with branches in South Africa, France, Russia, China, India, California and Washington.

ABC Inc. has succeeded in making partnership with KONAMI, NORDEUS, NVIDIA and GLU. We are looking foward to bring good gaming quality to you.
